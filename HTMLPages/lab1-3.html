The section contatiners are used to group other elements together, usually for positing purposes so we will use them more in the later leassons. Elements may also be grouped together in a section to so that the area of the page grouped by the section can be formatted with the same style, or background color such as this example.

Article 2The articles do not necessarily have to go inside the section, however if the articles are to be formatted into a single container, for example they need to be positioned or sized together, you can achieve this by placing both single section and formatting the section. The articles will inherit the formatting applied to the section.
